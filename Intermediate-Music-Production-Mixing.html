Intermediate - Music Production & Mixing

This intermediate-level course is designed for students who have a foundational understanding of music production and DAW signal flow and want to develop their skills in music production, editing, and mixing. Students will explore intermediate techniques in signal processing, dynamic control, EQ, and spatial effects to enhance clarity, depth, and balance in their mixes. The course covers multi-track session management, automation, vocal and instrument processing, and professional workflow strategies. Students will learn sound design techniques to create original music with the use of MIDI keyboards and virtual instruments. Through hands-on projects, students will refine their critical listening skills and gain the technical expertise needed to produce professional-quality music.

Course Highlights

  • Develop advanced skills in signal processing, dynamic control, EQ, and spatial effects.
  • Master multi-track session management and automation for more efficient workflows.
  • Learn to process vocals and instruments like a pro.
  • Create original music using MIDI keyboards and virtual instruments for sound design.
  • Hands-on projects to refine critical listening skills and professional music production techniques.

Required Hardware

  • MacBook Pro : A reliable, powerful computer is essential for running Pro Tools and handling audio software efficiently.

  • Mixing Headphones : High-quality headphones are necessary for accurate audio monitoring during the recording, editing, production, and mixing processes.

    Closed-back options: Beyerdynamic DT 770, Audio-Technica ATH-M50X, or similar models.
    Perfect for isolation, effectively blocking out outside noise. They are typically a more durable headphone.

    Open-back options: Sennheiser HD 650, Beyerdynamic DT-770 PRO, or similar models.
    Provide a more accurate sound ideal for critical listening and mixing. However, they are limited to use in quiet spaces and can be more fragile.

  • iLok : A USB device that is required for managing and storing licenses for Pro Tools and various plugins.

Recommended Hardware

  • Computer keyboard with numeric keypad
  • Computer Mouse
  • MIDI Keyboard: Recommended
Certification:
  • Upon successful completion of the course and assessments, students will receive a certificate of completion. This certificate is a valuable addition to your portfolio and can help demonstrate acquired skills in audio engineering, which may be useful for pursuing further education or career opportunities in the field.
