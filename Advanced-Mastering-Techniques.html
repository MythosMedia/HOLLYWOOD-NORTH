Advanced Mastering Techniques

This advanced-level course focuses on the final stage of audio production: mastering. Students will learn advanced EQ, multiband compression, stereo enhancement, and loudness optimization techniques to prepare tracks for commercial release. The curriculum also covers critical listening, metering, and mastering for various formats, including streaming and broadcast. Through real-world scenarios and hands-on projects, students will gain the precision and artistic approach required to deliver polished masters.

What You’ll Learn

  • Advanced mastering techniques for professional-quality final products
  • Stereo enhancement, multiband compression, and loudness optimization
  • Mastering for different media formats: streaming, vinyl, and broadcast
  • Critical listening skills and understanding industry loudness standards

Required Hardware

  • MacBook Pro : Handles the demanding processing needs of advanced mastering
  • Mixing Headphones : For precise monitoring and evaluation (e.g., Sennheiser HD 650, Beyerdynamic DT-770 PRO)
  • iLok : A USB dongle required for managing and storing licenses for Pro Tools and various plugins used in the production process.

Recommended Hardware

  • Keyboard with Keypad
  • Computer Mouse
Certification:
  • Upon successful completion of the course and assessments, students will receive a certificate of completion. This certificate can be added to your portfolio to showcase your skills and proficiency in audio post-production or music production, aiding in career opportunities or further education in the industry.
